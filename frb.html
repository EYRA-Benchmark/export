Fast Radio Burst Detection

A benchmark comparing software packages that detect fast radio bursts (FRBs)

To our knowledge, this is the first benchmark for comparing software used to search for Fast Radio Bursts in radio telescope data. In 2019, Liam Connor (UvA) organized a workshop to discuss the benchmark with the international community, following FRB 2019 in Amsterdam. This benchmark is a collaborative effort of the University of Amsterdam, ASTRON, the Netherlands eScience Center, SURF, and the international FRB search community.

Contact person

For more information about this benchmark, contact Liam Connor.
